What can this web app do?

  • Empower you to promote your content alongside popular videos.
  • Double your views by sharing 2 of your videos in 1 link.
  • Unionize content sharing instead of starving your YT channel by going on strike.
  • Compare perspectives side-by-side.

Here's the deal.
Online content creators such as YouTubers are unionizing to push platforms to treat them fairly.

Unfortunately, going on strike by pausing production of videos hurts themselves, not just the platforms.

Instead of modifying how often YouTubers produce content, this tool modifies how their videos are distributed

  • Share 2 videos side-by-side to unionize their content creators virtually.
  • Swap which video is muted by tapping the speaker button

Algorithms setup by elites filter what you can find to watch

  • Biased towards one perspective
  • Shows you more of what you've already engaged with - Filter Bubble

With Dyad, you can share two different perspectives side-by-side - Bypassing algorithmic filters.

You can instantly promote your content alongside more popular content to get more exposure

  • Copyright strikes are unlikely because content is embedded not copied
  • Both content creators can still monetize their videos as usual
